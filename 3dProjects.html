The Article

The first 3d model we decided to make was an Eiffel Tower. This is because we already had a template for it and we thought it would be a pretty easy building for Tatum to learn on.

Next we decided to make our own custom 3d model.

We decided to make the Big Nickel because it is one of sudburys famous landmarks.

The 3d Pen that we used is amazing. With it you can make anything you can dream. From a bridge to a house all possible.

To use the 3d Pen you must have two things. One a power source and two filament. There are two types of filament, Abs and Pla.

3doodlers sell for about $120 and 3d Pen 2 usually sells for 30-40 dollars. In my opinion the 3d Pen 2 is better because the 3doodler only works with custom filament which costs about 4 times as much as the regular filament.
